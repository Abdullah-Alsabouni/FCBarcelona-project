Histroy

* Football Club Barcelona is a football club located in the city of Barcelona in the autonomous region of Catalonia, Spain.


* It was founded in 1899 under the leadership of Joan Gamper by a Swiss, British and Spanish group.


* The slogan of the club representing the Catalonia region is the sentence "Més que un club".
